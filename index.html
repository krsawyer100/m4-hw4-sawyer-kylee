About

Jason Mraz is an American singer, songwriter, and guitarist. He was born on June 23, 1977 in Mechanicsville, Virginia. His music falls into the genres Folk-pop, surf rock, pop rock, and blue-eyed soul. Jason Mraz has won two Grammy Awards, two Teen Choice Awards, one People's Choice Award, and a Hal David Songwriters Hall of Fame Award (Wikipedia, n.d.).
